HOW TO PURCHASE SOLGR ?!

  1. Using your preferred exchange, purchase Solana. Coinbase, Binance, and Kraken are all great options but any exchange that offers Solana (SOL) will do.
  2. If you don't already have one, create a Solana wallet Such as Phantom or SolFlare where you can send the Solana that you purchased from the exchange. If you only have access via mobile device, Trust Wallet is a great option.
  3. Next you need to use a Decentralized Exchange (DEX) to convert the Solana tokens into (SOLGR) tokens Sites such as Raydium, Orca, or Step will all work. Just follow the site-specific prompts to connect your Solana wallet to their DEX service.
  4. Once your wallet is connected, you can now swap between SOL and SOLGR. Remember to retain some SOL in your wallet to account for any transaction fees when swapping between tokens.
  5. Congratulations! You are all set! Welcome to the Solana ecosystem and Sol Golden Retriever Family!

    6. If you are having any issues purchasing SolGR, creating a SOL wallet, or connecting to a DEX to convert your tokens. Feel free to reach out to our community on Discord
